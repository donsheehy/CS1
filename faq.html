  1. Will a university level course be too difficult for my student?

  1. If it's an introductory course, will it be too easy for my student?

  1. Will my student receive college credit for this course?

Individual colleges decide which courses can be used for transfer credits. Usually, there is a process whereby the syllabus of the course is examined and compared to the comparable course offered at the college. This class will meet and likely exceed the level of an introductory course offered at most colleges. I will happily write a letter of explanation to accompany the syllabus if you submit it for transfer credit.
